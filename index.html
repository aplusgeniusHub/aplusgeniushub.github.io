🚀 Welcome to Finyx

Finyx is your comprehensive personal finance management solution. This guide will help you get started and make the most of all features.

Quick Setup

  1. Launch Finyx on your Mac, iPhone, or iPad
  2. Create your first account by tapping the "+" button
  3. Choose account type (Cash, Bank, Credit Card, Digital Wallet, or Investment)
  4. Set your initial balance and customize with an icon and color
  5. Start tracking your financial journey!
💡 Pro Tip: Start by adding all your existing accounts to get a complete picture of your finances. You can always add more accounts later.

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides a quick snapshot of your financial health:

  • Total Balance: Combined balance across all active accounts
  • Monthly Summary: Income vs. expenses for the current month
  • Quick Stats: Top spending categories and recent transactions
  • Goals Progress: Visual indicators of your savings goals
  • Upcoming Bills: Reminders for bills due soon

💰 Managing Accounts

Account Types

Finyx supports five different account types to match your real-world finances:

  • 💵 Cash: Physical money, petty cash
  • 🏦 Bank: Checking and savings accounts
  • 💳 Credit Card: Credit cards and lines of credit
  • 📱 Digital Wallet: PayPal, Venmo, Apple Pay balance
  • 📈 Investment: Stocks, bonds, retirement accounts

Creating an Account

  1. Tap the + button in the Accounts section
  2. Enter a descriptive name (e.g., "Chase Checking", "Emergency Fund")
  3. Select the account type
  4. Set the current balance
  5. Choose your preferred currency (default: USD)
  6. Customize with an icon and color for easy identification
  7. Tap Save

Editing & Managing Accounts

  • Edit: Tap on any account to view details and make changes
  • Deactivate: Turn off accounts you no longer use without losing history
  • View History: See all transactions associated with an account
  • Balance Adjustment: Manual corrections for reconciliation
💡 Best Practice: Use distinct colors and icons for each account type to quickly identify them at a glance. Keep account names clear and consistent.

📊 Tracking Transactions

Adding a Transaction

  1. Tap the + New Transaction button
  2. Select Income or Expense
  3. Choose the account to record the transaction from
  4. Enter the amount
  5. Select or create a category
  6. Add a description (optional but recommended)
  7. Set the date (defaults to today)
  8. Attach a photo of receipt (optional)
  9. Add tags for better organization (optional)
  10. Tap Save

Transaction Categories

Built-in categories include:

  • 🍔 Food & Dining: Groceries, restaurants, coffee shops
  • 🚗 Transportation: Gas, public transit, ride-sharing
  • 🛍️ Shopping: Clothing, electronics, household items
  • 🎬 Entertainment: Movies, concerts, hobbies
  • 📄 Bills & Utilities: Rent, electricity, internet
  • ❤️ Healthcare: Medical expenses, pharmacy, insurance
  • 📚 Education: Tuition, books, courses
  • 💰 Salary: Paychecks, bonuses
  • 💼 Business: Business income and expenses
  • 📈 Investment: Dividends, capital gains
  • 🎁 Gift: Gifts given or received

Custom Categories

Create your own categories to match your unique spending patterns:

  1. Go to Settings → Categories
  2. Tap Add Category
  3. Name your category
  4. Choose an icon and color
  5. Select type (Income or Expense)

Editing & Deleting Transactions

  • Edit: Tap on any transaction to modify details
  • Delete: Swipe left on a transaction and tap Delete
  • Duplicate: Long press to create a copy for recurring expenses
  • Search: Use the search bar to find specific transactions
  • Filter: Filter by date range, category, or account
💡 Pro Tip: Log transactions as they happen for the most accurate financial picture. Use the quick-add feature for faster entry of frequent purchases.

🎯 Goals & Budget Management

Creating Financial Goals

  1. Navigate to the Goals section
  2. Tap New Goal
  3. Give your goal a name (e.g., "Emergency Fund", "Vacation to Japan")
  4. Set your target amount
  5. Choose a target date
  6. Add an initial contribution (optional)
  7. Select an icon and color
  8. Tap Create Goal

Contributing to Goals

  • Tap on a goal to view details
  • Tap Add Contribution
  • Enter the amount you're contributing
  • Track your progress with the visual indicator

Setting Up Budgets

  1. Go to the Budgets section
  2. Tap Create Budget
  3. Select a category to budget for
  4. Set your spending limit for the month
  5. Choose alert thresholds (e.g., 80%, 100%)
  6. Tap Save

Monitoring Budgets

  • Visual Progress: Color-coded bars show spending vs. budget
  • Alerts: Receive notifications when approaching limits
  • Monthly Reset: Budgets automatically reset each month
  • Historical Data: Compare month-to-month performance

Goal Strategies

💡 Smart Strategy: Set up multiple goals with different priorities:
  • Emergency Fund: 3-6 months of expenses (High Priority)
  • Debt Payoff: Target high-interest debts first
  • Retirement: Long-term wealth building
  • Short-term: Vacation, gadgets, experiences

Budget Categories

Common budget categories to consider:

  • Essential: Housing, utilities, groceries (50-60% of income)
  • Transportation: Gas, maintenance, insurance
  • Savings: Emergency fund, retirement (20% of income)
  • Discretionary: Entertainment, dining out (20-30%)
  • Debt Repayment: Credit cards, loans

🚀 Advanced Features

Investment Portfolio Management

Track and manage your investment portfolio with detailed analytics:

Adding Investments
  1. Navigate to Investments section
  2. Tap Add Investment
  3. Enter investment name (e.g., "Apple Stock", "S&P 500 ETF")
  4. Select investment type (Stocks, Bonds, Mutual Funds, ETF, Crypto, Real Estate)
  5. Enter purchase details:
    • Quantity/Shares
    • Purchase price
    • Purchase date
    • Fees/commissions
  6. Link to an investment account
  7. Save and track performance
Investment Analytics
  • Portfolio Overview: Total value, gains/losses, and allocation
  • Performance Tracking: ROI and percentage returns
  • Asset Allocation: Pie charts showing diversification
  • Historical Performance: Track value over time
  • Dividend Tracking: Record dividend income automatically
💡 Investment Tip: Regularly update investment values to track accurate performance. Set a weekly or monthly reminder to update prices.

Loan & Debt Management

Comprehensive loan tracking and payment planning:

Creating a Loan
  1. Go to Loans section
  2. Tap Add Loan
  3. Enter loan details:
    • Loan name (e.g., "Student Loan", "Car Loan")
    • Principal amount
    • Interest rate (annual)
    • Loan term (months/years)
    • Start date
  4. Choose loan type (Personal, Mortgage, Auto, Student, Business)
  5. Set payment frequency (Monthly, Bi-weekly, Weekly)
  6. Review payment schedule and total interest
Loan Features
  • Amortization Schedule: See principal vs. interest breakdown
  • Payment Tracking: Mark payments as completed
  • Extra Payments: Calculate impact of additional payments
  • Payoff Projection: Estimated payoff date and savings
  • Multiple Loans: Compare and prioritize debt payoff strategies

Bill Management & Reminders

Never miss a payment with smart bill tracking:

Setting Up Bills
  1. Navigate to Bills section
  2. Tap Add Bill
  3. Enter bill information:
    • Bill name (e.g., "Electric", "Netflix", "Rent")
    • Amount (can be variable or fixed)
    • Due date
    • Category
  4. Set recurring pattern:
    • Monthly (most common)
    • Weekly
    • Bi-weekly
    • Quarterly
    • Yearly
    • Custom
  5. Link to payment account
  6. Enable reminder notifications
Bill Features
  • Payment History: Track when bills were paid
  • Upcoming Bills: Dashboard showing bills due soon
  • Auto-record: Automatically create transactions when marked paid
  • Variable Bills: Update amounts for utilities that vary
  • Notifications: Custom reminders (1 day, 3 days, 1 week before)

Reports & Analytics

Gain insights into your financial patterns:

Available Reports
  • Spending by Category: Pie charts and bar graphs showing where money goes
  • Income vs. Expenses: Monthly comparison trends
  • Net Worth Tracker: Track total assets minus liabilities over time
  • Cash Flow Analysis: Detailed income and expense flow
  • Budget Performance: Compare actual spending vs. budgeted amounts
  • Goal Progress: Track savings goal achievement rates
  • Account Balances: Historical balance trends for all accounts

Data Management

Export & Backup
  • CSV Export: Export transactions for use in spreadsheets
Import Data
  • CSV Import: Import bank statements or data from other apps
  • Bulk Import: Add multiple transactions at once

Settings & Customization

Preferences
  • Default Currency: Set your primary currency
  • Date Format: Customize date display preferences
  • Notification Settings: Customize reminder frequency and timing
  • Security: Enable Face ID/Touch ID protection
Appearance
  • Theme: Light, Dark, or Auto (follows system)
  • Accent Colors: Customize app color scheme
  • Dashboard Layout: Rearrange widgets and cards
  • Chart Types: Choose preferred visualization styles
💡 Power User Tip: Use keyboard shortcuts on macOS for lightning-fast entry:
  • ⌘ + N - New transaction
  • ⌘ + B - New budget
  • ⌘ + G - New goal
  • ⌘ + F - Search transactions
  • ⌘ + , - Open settings

Recurring Transactions and Bills

Automate your regular income and expenses:

  1. Create a transaction as usual
  2. Enable "Make Recurring"
  3. Set frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
  4. Choose end date or make it ongoing
  5. Finyx will automatically create these transactions on schedule

Privacy & Security

Your financial data is protected:

  • Local Data: All data stored locally on your devices
  • iCloud Sync: End-to-end encrypted cloud sync (optional)
  • Biometric Lock: Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • Auto-lock: App locks after period of inactivity
  • No Ads: No third-party tracking or advertisements
  • No Account Required: Use the app without creating an online account

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is my financial data secure?

A: Yes! All your data is stored locally on your devices. Optional iCloud sync uses end-to-end encryption. We never see or have access to your financial information.

Q: Does Finyx sync across devices?

A: Yes, Finyx uses iCloud to seamlessly sync your data across your Mac, iPhone, and iPad. All your information stays current on every device.

Q: Can I import data from other apps?

A: Yes, Finyx supports CSV import for transactions. You can import bank statements and data from other finance apps.

Q: Is there a subscription fee?

A: Finyx operates on a simple monthly subscription model. One affordable fee gives you access across all three platforms - macOS, iPhone, and iPad.

Q: Does Finyx connect to my bank?

A: Finyx focuses on manual entry and privacy. It does not connect directly to banks, giving you complete control and security over your financial data.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

In Short

  • Your data stays on your device. Finyx stores info locally using SwiftData—unless you enable iCloud sync.
  • We can't see your financial information. We do not collect, transmit, or access your personal financial data.
  • No tracking, no ads, no data selling. We do not share or monetize personal data.
  • Works offline. You can use the app without internet access; iCloud sync is optional.
  • You control everything. Export, delete, or manage your data anytime.

Data Storage

All financial data created in Finyx is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not collect, store, or transmit your data to our servers. If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data will sync via Apple's iCloud infrastructure and remains encrypted in transit and at rest.

What Data You Store

Finyx stores the following data on your device when you create it:

  • Financial accounts and balances
  • Income and expense transactions
  • Bills and payment schedules
  • Loans and payment history
  • Financial goals and budgets
  • Investment information
  • Custom categories, tags, and notes

Security

Your data is protected by device-level security (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). Data stored on the device is encrypted using the operating system's encryption. Only someone with access to your unlocked device or iCloud account can access your synced data.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synchronized across your Apple devices using Apple’s secure iCloud infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data. You may disable iCloud sync at any time in the app settings.

Import & Export

When importing (for example CSV bank statements), files are processed locally on your device. When exporting, files are saved to a location you choose. We do not upload imported or exported data to our servers.

What We DON'T Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share data with advertisers.
  • We do not track your behaviour for analytics or marketing.
  • We do not require creating an account to use the app.
  • We do not use third-party trackers by default.

Your Rights

You control your data. You can:

  • Access all stored data on your device
  • Export data in CSV format
  • Delete individual records or all data
  • Disable optional features (iCloud, notifications)

Permissions

Finyx may request the following permissions (optional):

  • File Access: For importing and exporting files.
  • Notifications: For bill reminders (optional).
  • iCloud: For device sync (optional).

All permissions can be revoked via system Settings.
